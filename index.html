About Me

Dedicated to Healthcare Excellence

I am a passionate healthcare professional with dual qualifications from Nigeria and the United Kingdom, committed to delivering exceptional patient care and advancing health education. Grounded in my faith as a devoted Christian, I approach each day with compassion, integrity, and a calling to serve.

Currently serving at Countess of Chester Hospital in the United Kingdom, I bring a unique blend of clinical expertise and public health knowledge to every patient interaction. My journey in healthcare spans multiple countries and disciplines, allowing me to provide culturally competent and evidence-based care.

As a certified BLS and ACLS Provider, I am equipped to handle critical care situations with confidence and precision. My role as a Certified Peer Educator Trainer allows me to mentor and train the next generation of healthcare professionals.
